Dataset Descriptions

This is a direct subset of the ProteomeTools dataset with computed iRTs instead of retention times. The total data contains ~27.200 peptides and is mainly useful for teaching purposes - Training: Containing 27.160 peptides - Validation: Containing 6.800 peptides - Testing: Containing 6.000 peptides - Train/val: Containing 27.200 peptides.
